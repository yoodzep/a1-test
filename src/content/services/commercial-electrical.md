## Overview

A1 Electrical provides reliable commercial electrical services to businesses throughout Dunedin and Otago. We understand that downtime costs money, so we work efficiently and can schedule work to minimise disruption to your operations.

## Commercial Services

- **Office fit-outs** - Power, data, and lighting for workspaces
- **Retail installations** - Display lighting, point-of-sale power, signage
- **Hospitality** - Kitchen equipment, bar installations, ambient lighting
- **Warehouse and industrial** - Three-phase power, machinery connections
- **Healthcare facilities** - Compliant installations for medical environments

## What We Provide

### New Fit-Outs
Complete electrical design and installation for new commercial spaces. We work with your designers and builders to deliver on time and on budget.

### Upgrades and Additions
Expand your electrical capacity, add new circuits, or upgrade to more efficient lighting without disrupting your business.

### Maintenance Programs
Preventive maintenance to keep your electrical systems reliable. Scheduled inspections, testing, and repairs before problems occur.

### Emergency Response
When electrical issues threaten your business operations, we respond quickly to minimise downtime.

All commercial work is completed to NZ standards with full documentation. We're SiteWise certified with Gold status, demonstrating our commitment to health and safety on commercial sites.
