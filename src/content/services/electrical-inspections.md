## Overview

Whether you're buying a property, renting out a home, or need to meet insurance requirements, A1 Electrical provides thorough electrical inspections with detailed reports. Our Master Electricians identify issues before they become problems.

## Inspection Types

### Pre-Purchase Inspections
Before buying a property, know what you're getting into. We assess the electrical system's condition, identify any safety issues, and estimate costs for necessary upgrades.

### Landlord Compliance Inspections
Ensure your rental property meets healthy homes and electrical safety standards. We check all fixed electrical installations and provide documentation for your records.

### Insurance Inspections
Some insurance companies require electrical inspection reports for older properties. We provide comprehensive assessments that meet insurer requirements.

### Safety Audits
Regular safety inspections for commercial properties, workplaces, and high-risk environments.

## What We Inspect

- Switchboard condition and capacity
- Wiring age and condition
- Earthing and bonding
- Safety switches (RCDs)
- Power point and switch condition
- Light fittings and circuits
- Smoke alarm compliance
- Outdoor electrical installations

## Our Reports Include

- Overall condition assessment
- Safety issues identified
- Code compliance status
- Photographic evidence
- Prioritised recommendations
- Cost estimates for repairs
- Certification where applicable

## Quality Assurance

All inspections are conducted by registered electricians following industry standards. Reports are clear, thorough, and provided promptly to meet your deadlines.
