## Overview

Transform your home with smart technology and extend your living space outdoors. A1 Electrical installs modern automation systems and outdoor electrical solutions that enhance convenience, security, and enjoyment of your property.

## Smart Home Solutions

- **Smart lighting** - Control lights from your phone or voice
- **Automated switches** - Timer and sensor-controlled lighting
- **Home automation** - Integrated control of multiple systems
- **Security wiring** - Camera and alarm system installation
- **Network cabling** - Structured data cabling for reliable connectivity

## Outdoor Electrical

- **Garden lighting** - Pathway, feature, and security lighting
- **Deck and patio power** - Convenient outdoor outlets
- **Spa and pool electrical** - Safe installation for water features
- **Shed and garage power** - Workshop and storage lighting and power
- **Gate and security** - Electric gate motors and intercoms
