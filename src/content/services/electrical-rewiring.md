## Overview

Older homes often have electrical systems that can't safely meet modern demands. A1 Electrical provides complete rewiring services and switchboard upgrades to bring your property up to current safety standards while increasing capacity for today's electrical needs.

## Signs You May Need Rewiring

- Flickering lights or frequent blown fuses
- Two-pin power outlets (ungrounded)
- Fabric-covered or rubber-insulated wiring
- Burning smells or warm switches
- Frequent circuit breaker trips
- Home built before 1970

## Our Rewiring Services

- **Complete house rewiring** - Replace all old wiring with modern cable
- **Switchboard upgrades** - Modern circuit breakers and RCD protection
- **Capacity upgrades** - Increase electrical supply for modern demands
- **Earthing upgrades** - Proper grounding for safety
- **Meter board upgrades** - Work with power companies on supply upgrades

## Switchboard Upgrades

Even if your wiring is sound, an outdated switchboard can be a safety concern. Modern switchboards with RCD protection can save lives by cutting power in milliseconds if a fault occurs.
