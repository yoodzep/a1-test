## Overview

Building a new home or commercial space? A1 Electrical provides comprehensive electrical fit-out services from the ground up. We work closely with builders, architects, and homeowners to ensure your electrical systems are designed right from the start.

## What We Provide

- **Design consultation** - Work with you to plan electrical layouts that suit your lifestyle and budget
- **Full electrical installation** - All wiring, switchboards, power points, and lighting
- **Smart home integration** - Pre-wire for automation, security systems, and future technology
- **Energy efficiency** - LED lighting, efficient circuits, and solar-ready installations
- **Compliance certification** - All work certified and compliant with NZ standards

## Our Process

1. **Initial consultation** - Discuss your requirements and review plans
2. **Electrical design** - Create detailed electrical plans
3. **First fix** - Install cables and backing boxes during construction
4. **Second fix** - Install switches, sockets, and fixtures after plastering
5. **Final inspection** - Test all systems and provide compliance certificate

## Why Choose A1 Electrical

With over 40 years of experience in new builds, we understand the importance of getting it right the first time. Our team coordinates seamlessly with other trades to ensure your project stays on schedule.

## Quality Assurance

All new build installations come with full compliance certification and our workmanship guarantee. We use quality materials and follow best-practice installation methods for long-lasting results.
