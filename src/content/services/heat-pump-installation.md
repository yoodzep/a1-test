## Overview

Dunedin's climate demands reliable heating solutions. A1 Electrical supplies and installs quality heat pumps and ventilation systems to keep your home comfortable and healthy throughout the year.

- **Supply and install** - Quality heat pumps from leading brands
- **Wall-mounted units** - Ideal for living areas and bedrooms
- **High-wall and floor-console options** - Suit different room layouts
- **Multi-split systems** - One outdoor unit serving multiple rooms
- **Sizing advice** - Recommend the right capacity for your space

## Ventilation Solutions

- **Bathroom exhaust fans** - Remove moisture and prevent mould
- **Kitchen rangehoods** - Proper ventilation for cooking areas
- **Home ventilation systems** - Positive pressure systems for drier homes
- **Heat recovery ventilation** - Energy-efficient fresh air systems

## Why Heat Pumps?

Heat pumps are one of the most efficient ways to heat your home, providing both heating and cooling. Modern inverter heat pumps can deliver 3-4 units of heat for every unit of electricity used.
