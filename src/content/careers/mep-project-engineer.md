## About the role

You will bridge design and field execution for MEP scopes, keeping coordination tight and installations aligned with schedule and quality standards.

## What you’ll do

You’ll review drawings and submittals, run coordination with trades, and support procurement and long-lead planning. You’ll assist with commissioning prep and keep teams informed of progress and risks.

## Who you are

You’re detail-oriented, collaborative, and comfortable with both technical plans and field realities. You communicate clearly and keep stakeholders aligned.

## Why join us

You’ll shape how critical systems come together, working with teams that value proactive coordination and reliable turnover.
