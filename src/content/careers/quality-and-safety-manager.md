## About the role

You will own safety and quality programs across active sites, ensuring teams work safely and deliver to spec. You’ll coach crews, run audits, and keep compliance documentation tight.

## What you’ll do

You’ll conduct trainings, inspections, and incident reviews. You’ll partner with site leaders to enforce standards, resolve issues quickly, and maintain accurate reporting for clients and regulators.

## Who you are

You’re proactive, detail-driven, and confident in the field. You communicate clearly, earn trust with crews, and uphold standards without slowing delivery.

## Why join us

You’ll make a visible impact on site culture and quality, helping teams work safely while delivering work that stands up to scrutiny.
