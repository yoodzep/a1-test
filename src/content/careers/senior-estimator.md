## About the role

You will lead estimating efforts that inform our go/no-go decisions and winning proposals. Your work will set budgets, identify risks, and give operations a clear starting point.

## What you’ll do

You’ll manage takeoffs, solicit vendor pricing, and build detailed estimates. You’ll recommend value engineering options, present bid strategies, and document assumptions for smooth handoff to project teams.

## Who you are

You’re precise, analytical, and calm under deadlines. You know how to interpret complex drawings, question gaps, and present numbers with confidence and clarity.

## Why join us

You’ll influence which projects we pursue and how we win them, working with a team that values accuracy, transparency, and strong handoffs to the field.
